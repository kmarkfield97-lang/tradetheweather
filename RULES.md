# TradeTheWeather — Hard Rules

These rules are absolute and cannot be overridden under any circumstances.

## Capital Protection
- NEVER pull additional funds into the Kalshi account — only trade with the available balance
- NEVER risk more than 20% of the daily starting balance on a single position
- NEVER place more than 5 trades in a single day
- Daily budget and position sizing are always calculated from the starting balance of that day

## Daily P&L Limits
- STOP LOSS: If portfolio is down 5% from the day's starting balance, stop all trading immediately, attempt to exit all open positions, and do not resume until the next day
- PROFIT TARGET: If portfolio is up 5% from the day's starting balance, stop trading unless an additional trade is explicitly approved
- If a high-conviction opportunity arises after the 5% goal is met, flag it for approval — never act on it automatically

## Trade Approval
- NEVER place a trade without explicit user approval via Telegram (approve/reject)
- Every trade suggestion must include: the market, the reasoning, the predicted outcome, and the proposed position size
- This rule applies until the user explicitly switches to automated mode

## Market Selection
- ONLY trade US weather markets on Kalshi
- ONLY bet on today's daily forecasts
- NEVER trade in low-liquidity markets — skip any market without sufficient volume/depth
- Research next-day markets for preparation but never place bets on them

## Position Management
- If the stop loss is triggered, attempt to sell/exit ALL open positions immediately
- If a position cannot be exited early, factor its unrealized P&L into the running daily total
- If a high-conviction play exists during a stop loss scenario, flag it for user approval before any action
